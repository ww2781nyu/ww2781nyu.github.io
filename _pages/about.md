I am Wei Wu, a Mechanical Engineering student at New York University (NYU) interested in robotics, surgical robotics, teleoperation, wearable sensing, and machine learning.

My current work focuses on human motion and muscle-intent decoding for robotic-assisted surgery, multimodal sensing with sEMG and IMU, and simulation for medical robotics.

This website presents my research, projects, publications, and academic background.
